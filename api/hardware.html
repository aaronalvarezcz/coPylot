Hardware

coPylot implements device adapters on top of the vendor provided drivers, APIs and SDKs as well as on top of the drivers and lower level APIs we developed. Within these adapters, coPylot also has many convenience methods. To enable developers/experimentalists who might want to use the same functionalities here we are sharing our hardware API:
